Fig swirl: Melt butter over medium heat in a saucepan. Add brown sugar and stir to dissolve. Half all the figs and toss in the saucepan with water and lemon juice. cook over medium heat, stirring frequantly, until you have chunky-jammy mixture. Add salt with one or two stirs, set aside and let cool completely.

Ice cream: in a small pot over medium heat, combign milk , and granulated sugar until is compleetly sissolved and the milk is just barely lukewarm. Whisk in egg yolk. Set mixture in the fridge and wait until the mixture is cold.

Using an ice cream machine, pour liquid into the frozen basin and process according to manufacturer instructions,i.e., let spin and thicken for 20 minutes before adding mascarpone, fig jam mixture, and the nuts. Contuine to process for +/_10 minutes. Pour semi-frozen mixture into a pyrex dish or glass tuppaware, Frozen at least 2 hours before serving.
